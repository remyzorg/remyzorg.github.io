Who am I

I'm Rémy El Sibaïe Besognet, a French Phd Student in computer science. I have a master's degree in programmation and algorithmic from Université Pierre et Marie Curie, Paris 6. I'm currently working at LIP6 in the same university. My advisor is Emmanuel Chailloux, professor at UPMC. The subject is Programmation Web Réactive dans un cadre typé statiquement pour l'orchestration de contenus mutltimédia riches (Reactive Web programming in a static typing context for orchestration of rich multimedia content). More generally, I'm an Ocaml programmer and I enjoy it for its typing, and functionnal features.

Research

The goal of my Phd thesis is to propose languages and tools to create interactive and rich web applications driven by the reactive and synchronous paradigm. My master's degree internship focused on the same subject. My work is a part of the Ubiquitous Content Framework (UCF) project.
